New breeds of malware

Here are two kinds malware that caught my attention recently, which I had never thought about:

  • World of Warcraft password stealing trojan (via Bruce Schneier), most likely to make money off of selling the virtual goods,
  • "Ransomware" (via Wired), encrypting valuable files on the infected machine and selling the decryption keys.

These fit the trend of the last couple of years, the rise of profit as a motive for attacks. Profit definitely changes the threat modelling picture: for example, the traditional threat of sensitive documents being leaked or deleted morphs into extortion. I guess the mitigation stays the same in that case though: make backups.
I wonder what other kind of wacky or imaginative attacks hackers are coming up with these days and we may not even know anything about yet.
