Matrix Reloaded review on Kuro5hin

Check out this witty review of the Matrix Reloaded on Kuro5hin.
I agree with some of the comments: the sequel doesn't compare to the original, especially because the dialogs aren't snappy enough and the Zion scenes don't bring much. The hype and high expectations certainly don't help either.
But it is still a good movie. The fight scenes are great, and I liked the Oracle scene and following encounter with Agent Smith as the dialogs' quality is closer to the original's.
